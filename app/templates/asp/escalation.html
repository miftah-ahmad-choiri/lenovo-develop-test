Raise and track escalations for part issues, delivery problems, and WO disputes.

Escalation Types

Escalations can be raised from the Part Received modal on the Dashboard when a part issue is detected.

Wrong Part Received
Delivered part does not match the WO part number or description
DOA — Dead on Arrival
Part arrived damaged or non-functional
Wrong Route / Delivery
Shipment routed to wrong ASP or location
Incomplete Shipment
Parcel received but items are missing from the order
