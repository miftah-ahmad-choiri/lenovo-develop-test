Ticket Management

Review, respond to, and close tickets submitted by ASPs.

Planned Features

The Ticket Management module will enable administrators to handle all ASP-submitted tickets in one place.

Ticket Queue
View all open tickets from ASPs, filter by status, category, and urgency
Assign & Respond
Assign tickets to admin team members and post responses
Escalation Handling
Prioritise DOA, wrong part, and SLA breach tickets
Resolution History
Full audit log of all ticket actions and responses

Ticket Management Coming Soon

This module is currently in development. Ticket handling, ASP communication thread, and resolution workflow will be available in a future release.
